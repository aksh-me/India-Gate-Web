Our Story

The region's original
Indian kitchen

For more than 25 years, family-owned India Gate has served fresh, authentic Indian cuisine to St. John's and the surrounding area. As the oldest Indian restaurant in the region, we're known for our quality and consistency, using only the freshest, most flavourful ingredients.

From perfectly cooked lamb and tandoori chicken to our ever-popular naan, there's something for everyone — whether you're new to Indian food or a seasoned veteran. Dine-in, takeout and catering available.
